About Our App

Rajasthan GK Tricks app is made to help the students who are preparing for various competitive exams in Rajasthan ( the largest state of India). This app provides many short tricks to learn and remember various General Knowledge topics related to Rajasthan. It provides easiest methods to learn General Knowledge topics for competitive and other examinations. You can also watch many videos to learn General Knowledge topics in a fun way. This app is made for Hindi medium students. In the first version of app there are more than 100 GK Tricks . We will add more GK Tricks in upcoming versions. There are many GK Tricks based on many topics related to - History, Geography, Politics, Economics etc These tricks are useful in various exams conducted by various government agencies like - RPSC , RBSE , RSSB etc. Features :- User friendly and easy to use interface Categorised GK Tricks GK Tricks video for difficult topics Easy to switch between tricks Helpful in many exams like - RAS Rajasthan Teacher Exams REET Rajasthan Police Exams PATWARI LDC Gram Sevak and many more...
